How it works

Simple, personal, no obligations

1
We get to know you A conversation — in person or by call. We want to understand what you offer and who your typical guest is. No forms, no pitches.
2
We introduce clients directly When a client needs what you offer, we introduce them personally. A warm introduction — not a directory listing.
3
You take it from there The client is yours. No commission, no markup, no interference. We ask only that you treat them well — our reputation depends on it.
4
We check in after We occasionally ask our clients how they found the experience. That feedback stays between us — it helps us refine who we recommend.

KIMANZI does not receive fees or commissions from partners. Our recommendations are based solely on quality.
