Client onboarding (arrival, pick up, first hours)

Arrival and pick up

The guest arrives in Nairobi after a long journey. The first hours matter. Your role is calm, reliable first contact within the agreed scope.

  • Pick up is planned in advance and coordinated via UrbanChill and the label
  • Pick up can be done via Uber
  • UrbanChill can also arrange a trusted local taxi when needed
  • This is always done in consultation, so nothing is forced
  • Pick up is fronted by us so you are not burdened with unnecessary costs

First contact and settling in

This is not entertainment. It is presence. You help the guest move safely, settle in, and reduce overwhelm.

  • Safe transfer from arrival point to accommodation
  • Calm check-in support and basic orientation (practical, short, clear)
  • Communication stays private and minimal (Signal for coordination)
  • If something feels unsafe, you step back immediately — UrbanChill supports that
Meals and essentials
Meals are optional. The guest is free to choose or skip. If meals or essentials are agreed, this is arranged in advance. You are not expected to front costs. Per-day agreements are paid out clearly.
After completion
After the assignment is completed and confirmed, your fee is transferred the next day. Payout is done in EUR. USD and KES references are indicative only.
One hard rule
Being on time is mandatory. If you do not show up for an agreed assignment, the assignment and future assignments stop immediately and the working relationship ends.
