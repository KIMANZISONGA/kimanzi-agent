Why KIMANZI Works With Women

Guests choose KIMANZI because they want peace, clarity and a warm human presence. Women in Kenya bring this naturally — calm, awareness, kindness and strength.

At KIMANZI, you are not taking a job. You’re joining a partnership built on trust and intuition.

But in Kenya, I met women who bring calm and clarity effortlessly. Women who protect, support and guide without forcing anything.

Being a Partner

A KIMANZI agent is not a worker. She is a partner — someone who notices details, reads situations and shares what matters.

You help us see opportunities: events, safe places, ideas and anything that can strengthen how we support guests.

We grow together — you, Stephen and the other agents. No pressure. No competition. Just women supporting each other.

Services

SOLO — A Soft Landing
This service is for the guest’s first hours in Kenya. You meet them with calm energy, help them move from the airport to their accommodation, and create a gentle welcome.

ADVENTURE — Three Days of Steady Support
You guide softly, help with transport, planning, meals if needed, and protect the guest from overwhelm.

Shopping Service
Before the guest arrives, you prepare gentle essentials: water, juice, fruit, snacks.

Meals & Simplicity

Meals are light and respectful. One meal during SOLO, up to two during ADVENTURE — only if the guest wants. Soft drinks only.

The Role

A KIMANZI agent brings calm, warmth and clarity to a guest’s first hours in Kenya.

You stay connected via Signal — calm, simple, private.

You help arrange safe Uber transport, create a soft landing, help with meals, planning, clarity.

You protect guests from overwhelm and support them gently, not forcefully.

Your wellbeing is always first. You step back immediately if something feels unsafe.

Everything the guest shares stays private. The client relationship always stays with KIMANZI.
