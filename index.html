The Role

A KIMANZI agent brings calm, warmth and clarity to a guest’s first hours in Kenya.

You stay connected with the guest through Signal — calm, simple, private. You help arrange safe Uber transport so her journey from the airport is smooth and predictable. No rushing, no confusion, no pressure.

When she arrives at the apartment, you create a soft landing. A drink, something light to eat, a moment to breathe. You check the essentials: does she feel safe, settled, grounded? You offer gentle clarity about the neighbourhood — where she can find food, where she can rest, and where she can move without noise or stress.

Your presence is warm but never intrusive. You guide with awareness, sharing only what is useful in the moment. You read situations quietly, and you protect the guest from overwhelm. You show possibilities, not instructions. You support, you do not push.

During an Adventure assignment, you offer steady companionship over several days. You help with planning and movement, explain how things work in Kenya, and prepare light meals if she wishes. Some guests want to talk, others want silence. You follow her rhythm — not the other way around.

You also notice opportunities, ideas and observations that help KIMANZI grow: safe cafés, calm areas, events, trusted people, moments of insight. You share what you see so that we continue building a service that feels human and thoughtful. We grow through your eyes as much as through the guest’s experience.

If anything feels unsafe or uncomfortable, you step back immediately. Not later, not after checking — immediately. Your wellbeing is non-negotiable. KIMANZI supports your decision without hesitation, always.

Everything the guest shares remains private. You offer safety, not stories. You hold boundaries with kindness and clarity. Professional distance keeps both you and the guest comfortable. The client relationship always stays with KIMANZI — this protects you and keeps expectations clear and simple.

In the end, your role is more than a task. You bring calm to a moment of arrival. You create clarity where everything is new. You help a woman feel safe in a foreign place. This is the heart of KIMANZI — quiet guidance, warm presence, and respect for every boundary.
