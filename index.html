Services

The Role

A KIMANZI agent is a woman who brings calm, warmth and clarity to a guest’s first hours in Kenya.

She stays connected via Signal: calm, simple, private.

She helps arrange safe Uber transport, creates a soft landing and supports with meals, planning and clarity.

She protects guests from overwhelm and supports them gently, never pushy.

Her wellbeing is always first. She steps back immediately if something feels unsafe, and KIMANZI supports that choice.

Everything the guest shares stays private. The client relationship always stays with KIMANZI.
