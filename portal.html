How payment works
💳
Pre-funded by the guest
Assignments only enter the pipeline after the guest has paid KIMANZI in full. Your fee is on our account before your day begins.
📸
Send invoice + receipts
Right after the assignment: photo of each receipt + your invoice to host@kimanzi.nl. No special form needed.
⚡
Paid within 2 days
Admin time, not financial time. Once receipts arrive and work is confirmed, payout runs via M-PESA — minutes to your account.
🧾
Expenses reimbursed
Fuel and lunch you advance on the day are reimbursed in full on the same invoice. No pre-approval needed.
