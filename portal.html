Concierge — workload guideline
The guideline is 6 active hours per guest, per month. Most of it is remote — messages, reservations, referrals. One in-person visit per month is included and reimbursable.
If a guest consistently runs over 6 hours, suggest an Extra Day instead. Concierge isn't unlimited consulting.
No hourly billing. The 6 hours is a workload guideline, not an invoicing basis — you invoice KSh 9,000 per active month regardless of whether the guest asks for more or less.
How payment works
💳
Pre-funded by the guest
Assignments only enter the pipeline after the guest has paid KIMANZI in full. Your fee is on our account before your day begins.
📱
Log receipts via the portal
Right after the assignment: open My Assignments in this portal, open the assignment, and add a photo of each receipt there. No email, no form — it counts toward your payout immediately.
⚡
Paid within 2 days
Admin time, not financial time. Once receipts arrive and work is confirmed, payout runs via M-PESA — minutes to your account.
🧾
Expenses reimbursed
Fuel and lunch you advance on the day are reimbursed in full on the same invoice. No pre-approval needed.
Annual indexing
📈
Reviewed every January, starting 2027
2026 rates are starting rates — no indexing this year. From 1 January 2027, fees adjust annually based on the average of Dutch and Kenyan inflation (CBS + KNBS) over the prior year, rounded to the nearest KSh 500. You'll hear about any change before 15 December, ahead of it taking effect.
Full indexing formula & sources tap to expand ▾
Step 1. In December, read the final annual CPI figures: CBS (Netherlands) and KNBS (Kenya).
Step 2. Average the two: (NL% + KE%) / 2 = indexing percentage.
Step 3. Apply to all KSh amounts, rounded to the nearest KSh 500.
Step 4. New amounts communicated to active hosts before 15 December, effective 1 January.
Sources: CBS → cbs.nl · Kenya National Bureau of Statistics → knbs.or.ke
